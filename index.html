Play Coup on the go – no internet required

How to play on a plane

  1. One player creates a mobile hotspot
  2. All others connect to that hotspot
  3. Open this app on all devices
  4. Host clicks Create Game, others click Join Game
  5. Exchange connection codes (QR or copy-paste)
  6. Enjoy Coup at 30,000 ft! ✈️

Works on local WiFi or when everyone loads the app first.
